# Complex Number

### Definition

```math
z = a + b\,i
```

```math
\begin{array}{cll}
z & & \small\text{Complex number} \\
a & & \small\text{Real part} \\
b & & \small\text{Imaginary coefficient} \\
i & \sqrt{-1} & \small\text{Imaginary unit}
\end{array}
```

#### Multiplication

```math
\begin{aligned}
z_1 z_2
&= (a+bi)(c+di) \\
&= (ac - bd) + (ad + bc) \, i
\end{aligned}
```

#### Conjugate

```math
\overline{z} = a - b\,i
```

Complex conjugate denoted using $\overline{z}$ or $z^*$ flips the sign of the imaginary part.

#### Magnitude (Modulus)

```math
|z| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2},
\qquad
|z|^2 = a^2 + b^2
```

#### Argument (Phase $\varphi$)

```math
\varphi = \arg(z) = \text{atan2}(b, a)
```

#### Eulerâ€™s Formula (Complex exponential)

```math
e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta
```

```math
e^{-i\theta} = \cos\theta - i\sin\theta
```
